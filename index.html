About Me

After 14 years working in accounting, I witnessed first-hand how the field is rapidly transforming with automation and AI. Tasks such as document processing, reconciliations and validations are increasingly automated — and I believe this shift is not a threat, but an opportunity.

My goal is to help make this transition viable, reliable and error‑free. With deep knowledge of accounting processes, I understand where mistakes happen, what needs to be validated, and how workflows can be optimized. Now, I’m applying this experience to build automated solutions that free time, reduce operational risk, and allow professionals to focus on what truly matters: supporting clients with strategic, healthy decisions for their business.

To prepare for this new path, I completed the AiDAPT Data Analysis certification at CEGID Academy, where I developed technical skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau and data‑driven problem solving. My projects focused on identifying the causes of employee attrition, understanding retention drivers, and designing insights that help companies reduce turnover and protect their investment in people.

Today, I’m transitioning into the tech field with a clear mission: to create automation and data solutions that increase efficiency, improve decision‑making, and generate meaningful impact.
